Portrait Photography

I shot photos of Taylor Graft a student at the University of South Carolina in a parking garage that had very dark and dim lighting. I used the lights on the ceiling for backlight with the camera flash for proper lighting. The goal was to capture her soft and sweet personality in a portrait she could use for a modeling agency.

Dae Product Photography

I used creative lighting in the studio to shoot product photography for Dae's signature shampoo and conditioner as well as other loved products from the line. After adjusting studio lighting and combining red and yellow gels, I was able to produce a vibrant mix of the two for a perfect background color.
